Tickets & Booking

How do I book tickets?

Simply browse our events, select the one you're interested in, choose your ticket quantity, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.

How will I receive my tickets?

After successful payment, you'll receive an email confirmation with:

  • PDF tickets attached to the email
  • QR codes for quick entry at the venue
  • A link to access your tickets in your account

Can I buy tickets for someone else?

Yes! During checkout, you can specify different attendee details. The tickets will still be sent to your email address, and you can forward them to the attendees.

What if I don't receive my tickets?

First, check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find them, log into your account to access your tickets directly, or contact our support team for assistance.

Payments & Fees

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

Credit/Debit Cards
Google Pay
Apple Pay

What are the booking fees?

7% processing fee is applied to each ticket purchase. This fee covers:

  • Secure payment processing
  • Platform maintenance and support
  • Ticket generation and delivery
  • Customer service

When will I be charged?

Your payment is processed immediately upon completing your booking. You'll receive a payment confirmation along with your tickets.

Safety & Security

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all transactions. Your payment details are processed by certified payment providers and are never stored on our servers.

How do you verify events and organizers?

All event organizers go through a verification process including:

  • Identity verification
  • Business registration checks (for companies)
  • Review of past event history
  • Ongoing monitoring of event quality and customer feedback

What if I suspect fraudulent activity?

If you notice any suspicious activity or have concerns about an event, please contact our security team immediately at security@jerseyevents.com. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.

For Event Organizers

How do I become an event organizer?

Register for a regular account, then upgrade to an organizer account by providing additional business information and completing our verification process. This includes providing business details, address, and agreeing to our organizer terms.

What fees do organizers pay?

Organizers receive payments minus the 7% platform fee. This means if a ticket is sold for £100, the organizer receives £93. All fees are transparent and shown during event creation.

When do I receive payment for ticket sales?

Payments are processed through Stripe Connect and are typically available within 2-3 business days after the event date. For recurring events, payments are processed weekly.

Refunds & Cancellations

What is the refund policy?

Refund policies are set by individual event organizers. Each event page displays its specific refund policy. Common policies include:

  • Full refund up to 7 days before the event
  • 50% refund up to 48 hours before the event
  • No refunds within 48 hours of the event

How do I request a refund?

Log into your account, go to "My Tickets," find the relevant booking, and click "Request Refund." Your request will be reviewed according to the event's refund policy.

What if an event is cancelled?

If an event is cancelled by the organizer, you'll receive a full refund including all fees. Refunds for cancelled events are processed automatically within 5-7 business days.

Account & Support

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through:

  • Email: support@jerseyevents.com
  • Phone: +44 (0) 1534 123456 (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm)
  • Live chat: Available on our website during business hours

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team. Please note that we may need to retain certain information for legal and tax purposes, but your personal data will be anonymized.
